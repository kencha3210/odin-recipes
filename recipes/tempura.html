Tempura Recipe

Description


Tempura (天ぷら or 天麩羅, tenpura) is a typical Japanese dish that usually consists of seafood and vegetables that have been coated in a thin batter and deep fried.

Ingredients


  • Tempura batter
  • Tempura Ingredients like shrimp
  • Oil for frying
  • Condiment

Steps

  1. Prepare Tempura batter
  2. Prepare ingredient
  3. heat the oil
  4. coat the ingredients
  5. fry the tempura
  6. drain excess oil
  7. serve
